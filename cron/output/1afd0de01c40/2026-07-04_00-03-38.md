## Top 5 LLM Fine-Tuning Papers on arXiv This Week

- **Compatibility-Aware Dynamic Fine-Tuning for Large Language Models**
  Published: 2026-04-22
  Abstract: Extends Dynamic Fine-Tuning with a compatibility signal derived from model likelihoods to suppress high-variance gradients from incompatible demonstrations, improving SFT stability and generalization without requiring a reward model.
  arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11206

- **Learning Rate Matters: Vanilla LoRA May Suffice for LLM Fine-tuning**
  Published: 2026-02-04
  Abstract: Through extensive hyperparameter sweeps across nine LoRA variants, demonstrates that vanilla LoRA with properly tuned learning rates achieves within 1–2% of more complex variants, suggesting that reported improvements often stem from suboptimal baselines rather than genuine methodological advances.
  arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04998

- **Evolution Strategies at Scale: LLM Fine-Tuning Beyond Reinforcement Learning**
  Published: 2025-09-29
  Abstract: First successful demonstration of evolution strategies for full-parameter fine-tuning of billion-parameter LLMs, outperforming RL-based methods in long-horizon reward tolerance, robustness, and resistance to reward hacking.
  arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.24372

- **LoRAFusion: Efficient LoRA Fine-Tuning for LLMs**
  Published: 2025-09-30
  Abstract: Introduces a graph-splitting kernel fusion method and adaptive multi-job batching scheduler for LoRA fine-tuning, achieving up to 1.96× end-to-end speedup over Megatron-LM and accepted at EuroSys 2026.
  arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.00206

- **Boosting Large Language Models with Mask Fine-Tuning**
  Published: 2025-03-27
  Abstract: Proposes Mask Fine-Tuning (MFT), a zero-weight-update fine-tuning paradigm that learns binary masks on model weights to break structural integrity and boost performance, achieving +2.70–4.15 IFEval gains on LLaMA models without modifying any weights.
  arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.22764
